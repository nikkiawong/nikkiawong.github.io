Asian American Students Association

As the Public Relations Chair for Asian American Students Association, I created a rebrand campaign for the organization, starting with a new logo. The new logo was designed to feel clean and professional as the original logo appeared outdated. I chose a fresh new color palette, as the organization did not already have a set brand color palette, to help streamline its brand identity. The new logo and other rebrand elements were implemented almost immediately after they were presented to the executive board.
