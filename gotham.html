Gotham Type Specimen Book

For my graphic design class I was assigned to create an informative book surrounding one typeface using a creative theme of my choosing. I selected Gotham as my typeface and the iconic American TV show F.R.I.E.N.D.S. as my theme, as Gotham is strongly tied to New York City, where the show is famously set.
