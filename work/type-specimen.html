Type Specimen Book

For my graphic design class, we were assigned to choose one font to showcase in a book designed around a theme of our choosing. I chose Gotham as my font and the iconic American TV show F.R.I.E.N.D.S. as my theme, based on the fact that Gotham is a font heavily associated with New York City where the show is famously set.
