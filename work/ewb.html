Engineers Without Borders

As part of a team of students in PhilADthropy, a 25-hour advertising volunteer event, I helped create a campaign and rebrand for University of Miami Engineers Without Borders. The aim of the project to create a campaign to attract new members to the organization, targeting those outside of engineering majors and design a new, fresh logo, color palette, and essential deliverables.
